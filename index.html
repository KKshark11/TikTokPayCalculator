How Much Does TikTok Pay Per 1,000 Views?

TikTok payouts vary depending on video length, watch time, audience location, engagement rate, and program eligibility. Short-form videos under 60 seconds typically earn between $0.02–$0.06 per 1,000 views under older creator fund models.

Longer videos over one minute may qualify for the Creator Rewards Program, where reported payouts in the United States range from $0.30–$1.00 per 1,000 views depending on performance metrics.

What Affects TikTok RPM?

  • Audience country (US views generally pay more)
  • Average watch time and retention
  • Video length eligibility
  • Engagement rate
  • Advertiser demand

Are These Earnings Guaranteed?

No. All calculations provided by this website are estimates based on publicly reported payout ranges. Actual earnings may vary depending on program eligibility, policy updates, and advertiser demand.
