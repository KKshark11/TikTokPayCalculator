How Much Does TikTok Pay Per 1,000 Views?

TikTok payouts vary depending on video length, audience location, watch time, engagement rate, and eligibility for monetization programs. Under older Creator Fund models, short videos under 60 seconds often earned between $0.02–$0.06 per 1,000 views.

Under the newer Creator Rewards Program, longer videos over one minute in high-value countries such as the United States may earn between $0.30–$1.00 per 1,000 views depending on retention and engagement.

How Much Does TikTok Pay For 10,000 Views?

With an average RPM of $0.50, a video reaching 10,000 views could generate approximately $5. Lower engagement or shorter videos may earn less, while strong retention and high advertiser demand may increase earnings.

How Much Does TikTok Pay For 1 Million Views?

At scale, earnings increase linearly. A creator earning $0.50 RPM could generate around $500 per 1 million views. Actual payouts vary based on performance metrics and monetization status.

What Affects TikTok RPM?

  • Audience country (US and UK typically pay more)
  • Average watch time and retention rate
  • Video length eligibility (over 1 minute preferred)
  • Engagement rate (likes, shares, comments)
  • Advertiser demand in your niche
